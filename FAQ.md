# Internal FAQ — Customer Support Agent


This FAQ contains answers to common customer questions about the product and account management.


---


## Account & Access


**How do I reset my password?**  

You can reset your password by clicking “Forgot password” on the login page and following the instructions sent to your email.


**I forgot which email I used to sign up. What should I do?**  

If you’re unsure which email you used, contact customer support for help verifying your account.


**Can I change the email address on my account?**  

Yes. You can update your email address from your account settings after signing in.


---


## Billing & Payments


**How do I update my payment method?**  

You can update your payment information in the billing section of your account settings.


**When will I be charged for my subscription?**  

Subscriptions are billed on a recurring monthly basis, starting from the date you first subscribed.


**What happens if a payment fails?**  

If a payment fails, we will notify you and retry the charge. Continued failures may result in temporary account restrictions.


---


## Subscriptions & Plans


**How do I upgrade or downgrade my plan?**  

You can change your subscription plan from your account settings at any time. Changes take effect immediately.


**Can I cancel my subscription?**  

Yes. You can cancel your subscription from your account settings. Your access will continue until the end of the current billing period.


---


## Product Usage


**Does the product store customer data?**  

The product stores only the data necessary to provide the service. Specific details may vary depending on usage.


**Is my data secure?**  

We take security seriously and follow industry best practices to protect customer data.


---


## Support & General Questions


**How can I contact customer support?**  

You can reach customer support through the help section of the product or by submitting a support request.


**What should I do if something isn’t working?**  

If you experience an issue, try refreshing the page or signing out and back in. If the issue persists, contact customer support.


---


## Important Notes


- This FAQ does not cover every possible question.

- For issues related to personal health or legal matters, users should consult a qualified professional.

- Some actions require direct assistance from customer support.
